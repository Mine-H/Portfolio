Together with learning the foundations for videogame rendering, we were asked to create a 3D environment in OpenGL, demonstrating hierarchical relationships in the placement of the objects, mapping of textures onto their surfaces, lighting, among other things.

C++ Seasons - November 2023

As our introduction to C++, we were asked to develop a small Command Prompt game. I decided to make an ASCII game where the player had to keep a tree alive by balancing between tending to it and collecting fruit from it to earn currency - all the while going through the story of the tree's caretaker's highs and struggles.

Python Order of Operations + Dice - Early 2024

HTML Pong - Mid-2022

As a personal challenge of mine, I decided to replicate the famous game Pong entirely in HTML/CSS/JS. All the operations are ran in JavaScript, and the code can easily be accessed via the inspector.

If you want to give it a try, the controls are: WS to move the left bar, Up and Down arrows for the right one, and Esc to toggle pause on the square.

HTML Floating Point Visualisation - Early-2022

In order to help my A-level classmates visualise how Floating Point works in the computer, I created a quick interactive website to showcase its functionality. The site was later used by the class that came after us for the same purpose, where I assisted the teacher in the explanation.
